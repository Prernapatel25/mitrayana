About Mitrayana

A trusted senior citizen help portal built on care and dignity.

OUR STORY

Built with Care, Trust & Compassion

As families become nuclear and children move to different cities or countries, many senior citizens are left to manage health, safety, and emotional needs alone.

Mitrayana was born to ensure that no elderly person feels helpless. We bring together verified service providers, healthcare assistance, and community support on one trusted platform.

Challenges Faced by Senior Citizens

Health Concerns

Regular medical care, emergencies, and medication management.

Daily Assistance

Help with household work, mobility, and daily routines.

Loneliness

Lack of companionship and emotional support.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower senior citizens by providing compassionate, reliable, and accessible support services. We ensure safety, independence, emotional well-being, and dignity at every stage of life.

  • Promote independent and dignified living
  • Provide trusted care and assistance
  • Offer emotional and social support
  • Ensure timely help and guidance

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a future where every senior citizen feels respected, secure, and connected. We aim to become India’s most trusted and innovative senior care platform.

  • Be India’s leading senior care platform
  • Use technology for smarter elder care
  • Build an inclusive support community
  • Ensure lifelong safety and well-being
